# MERGE STORM
> Numbers fall. You steer. Watch the stack merge into monster values.

## Short description (CrazyGames, ~140字)
Tap left or right to steer falling number blocks into a 5-column stack. Match values to merge them into bigger numbers — how high can you push your max?

## Full description
MERGE STORM is a one-thumb arcade stacker: number blocks drop straight down a 5-column board, and a single tap on the left or right half of the screen nudges the falling block into place. Land it on a matching value and the stack merges upward, chaining into satisfying combo bursts, screen shake, and a rising max-value counter that dominates the top of the screen. Miss your landing and the numbers pile up — overflow the columns and it's game over.

The pace ramps as your score climbs, turning a calm early stack into a frantic dodge-and-merge scramble. Every merge pops with particle bursts and a punchy synth chime, and your best score is tracked locally so each run is a fight against your own record. It's easy to pick up in three seconds and hard to put down once the streak starts building.

## Controls
- Tap the left half of the screen to steer the falling block left
- Tap the right half of the screen to steer the falling block right
- Tap once to start, tap again after game over to restart
